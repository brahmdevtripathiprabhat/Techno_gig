Indian Astrology

Indian astrology, known as Jyotish Shastra, is an ancient system rooted in the Vedas. It offers profound insights into personality, life paths, and cosmic influence. Let's explore its wonders!

Vedic Astrology

Based on the Vedas, this ancient system uses precise planetary positions to chart your janam kundali (birth chart) and predict life events.

Planets & Rashis

The nine planets (Navagrahas) and 12 zodiac signs (Rashis) influence every aspect of life, shaping personality and destiny.

Nakshatras

The 27 lunar constellations, or Nakshatras, provide deeper insights into emotional and spiritual well-being.

Dashas

Dashas are planetary periods that reveal when certain aspects of life are likely to flourish or face challenges.

Panchang

The Panchang is a Hindu calendar that combines lunar phases, tithis, and nakshatras for auspicious event planning.

Astrological Remedies

Remedies like gemstones, mantras, and rituals align cosmic energies to mitigate challenges and enhance positive influences.
