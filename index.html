Meet Parul Varshney

15+ Years of Trusted Astrological Guidance

Parul Varshney from Kota, Rajasthan, is a highly respected astrologer helping people navigate life's challenges. With an impressive educational background including B.Ed, MA, MBA, and a Ph.D., she blends academic excellence with deep astrological expertise. Specializing in Kundali reading, Marriage Compatibility, and a wide range of life issues such as career growth, health concerns, family disputes, and financial stability, Parul has become a trusted guide for her clients seeking clarity and solutions.

Her compassionate approach and accurate predictions have earned her a loyal clientele, who admire her practical remedies and life-transforming guidance. Many clients praise her for helping them find peace, success, and happiness through personalized astrological solutions.
